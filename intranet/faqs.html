Creative Team FAQs

There are a number of things that our team can support you with as outlined on the Creative Services intranet page, but there's still a bit of confusion about it, so to help we have listed some of the most frequently asked questions below.

If you can't find the answer to what your looking for here or on our intranet page, then please contact design@london.gov.uk and we'll see what we can do.

Q. Can you send me a logo?
A. We can provide Mayor of London, Supported by Mayor of London and London Assembly logos. You must complete this form before we can send it to you, and please make sure you send a PDF of any materials before publishing to design@london.gov.uk.
 
Q. How do I request design work?
A. If you have any design requests, you will need to fill in a briefing form. Please email the completed form to design@london.gov.uk.
 
Q. Do you have nice paper we can use to print on?
A. Sorry, no. We use the same photocopy paper that it is supplied by FM.
 
Q. Can you design a map of an area of London?
A. Cartography is a separate discipline which requires specialist software. The Intelligence team should be able to help. Please contact intelligence@london.gov.uk.
 
Q. Do you have any scissors / rubbers / hole punchers / staplers / pencils?
A. Most of our work is computer-generated, so chances are we wouldn't store these supplies. If you need stationery, ask your Business Support to purchase them for you.
 
Q. Can you design a report for us?
A. Designing a report can take many weeks. We need to make sure we are using our resource effectively, so we have created a Word template for you to use to lay out your report. You can request a copy of the template by emailing design@london.gov.uk. We have produced some guidance to help.
 
Q. Can you create a PowerPoint presentation for us?
A. We use standard industry design software. Our knowledge of PowerPoint is limited, therefore we won't be able to do this for you. However, there is a PowerPoint template available for you to use. For more information please visit our intranet page.
 
Q. I need an image of...
A. We have an image library which includes a large and growing collection of images you can browse, select and download. For access please contact your policy comms officer.
 
Q. Do you have a laminator that we can use?
A. Sorry no, but Facilities Management have one.
 
Q. Do you have a comb-binder?
A. The simple answer is no, we don't do any printing or finishing in-house.
 
Q. Can you embed a video into PowerPoint?
A. Afraid not, try Googling it... If no joy. speak to TG.
 
Q. Can you convert PDFs into Word documents or JPEGs?
A. If you need this done on a regular basis, then contact TG to see if they can give you access to Adobe Acrobat.
 
Q. Can you take my headshot photos?
A. Our photographer takes headshots of the Mayor and his advisors, Deputy Mayors, Assembly members and SMT members who do not already have their headshot on the website. Please contact caroline.teo@london.gov.uk if you require one.
 
Q. Can you take our team photos?
A. Yes, our photographer can take your team photos, as long as the Mayor/Deputy Mayor is included. Please contact caroline.teo@london.gov.uk.
 
Q. Can you take my passport photos?
A. Afraid not, best going to Snappy Snaps.

We hope this page has been helpful and answered your query. If you're still unsure and have looked through our intranet page, then please contact design@london.gov.uk and we'll see what we can do.
