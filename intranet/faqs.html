Creative Team FAQs

There is often a lot of confusion around what our team can support you with, so we have put together some of the most frequently asked questions. If you can't find the answer to what your looking for here or on the Creative Services intranet page, then please contact design@london.gov.uk.

Q. Do you have nice paper we can use to print on?
A. Afraid not, we use the same photocopy paper that it is supplied by FM.
Q. Can you design a map of an area of London?
A. Cartography is a separate discipline which requires specialist software, the Intelligence team should be able to help with this.
Q. Do you have any scissors / rubber / holepunch / stapler / pencils?
A. Most of our work is computer generated, so chances are we wouldn’t store these supplies. If you need stationary, ask your Business Support to purchase them for you.
Q. Can you design a report for us?
A. Designing a report can take many weeks for us, we need to make sure we are using our resource effectively, so we have created a Word template for you to use to lay out your report
Q. Can you create a nice PowerPoint presentation for us?
A. We use standard industry design software, our knowledge of PowerPoint is limited, there is a PowerPoint template available for you to use
Q. I need an image of…
A. We have a City Hall Image Library which includes a large and growing collection of images you can browse, select and download, for access please contact your policy comms officer
Q. Do you have a laminator that we can use?
A. Sorry no, but Facilities Management have one
Q. Do you have a comb-binder?
A. The simple answer is no, we don’t do any printing or finishing in-house
Q. Can you embed a video into PowerPoint?
A. Afraid not, try googling it, if no joy – speak to TG
Q. Can you convert PDFs into Word documents or JPEGs?
A. If you need this done on a regular basis, then contact TG to see if they can give you access to Adobe Acrobat

We can still help with the following:

Respond to design briefs

If you have any design requests, you will need to fill in a briefing form – please email design@london.gov.uk

Supply Word report template

If you need to publish a report, you can request a copy of the word template from: design@london.gov.uk – We have produced some guidance to help
